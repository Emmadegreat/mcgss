About MCGSS

Model Comprehensive Girls Secondary School Ugwuachara Abakaliki was established in the year 1980 by the then governor of the old Anambra state His Excellency Dr. Jim Ifeanyichukwu Nwobodo. The school is a single-sex school established to promote girls education.
It was a full boarding school until 2015 when the present government returned it to Day and Boarding. We have facilities and good conducive environment for learning, well equipped science (physics, Biology and Chemistry) Laboratory, good home economics management section, modern equipment introductory technology workshop. To learn interesting, we encourage our students to belong in at least one school club which includes debating society, press club, jet club and so on.
It is interesting to note that the wife of the current governor of Ebonyi State Chief Mrs Racheal Umahi is our Alumnus and other notable figures in the society. We are excellence in girls education and strive to maintain

Vision

We move with the dynamics of technology trends, building a strong solid base for the present future of our young women to achieve personal success in an ever changing world, we provide care and happiness, respect and adore achievements.

Mission

To be an excellent institution in girl child education and providing all supports for her to attain excellence in her  dream career.
